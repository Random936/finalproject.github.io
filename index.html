Take the test

Naturalization is the legal act or process by which a non-citizen of a country may acquire citizenship or nationality of that country. (Wikipedia) According to the US Citizenship and Immigration Services website, the naturalization exam is composed of an English and civics test. You are given two attempts to sucessfully pass. The English test will test your ability to read, write, and speak. You must read and write one out of three sentences correctly to pass. The civics test is an oral exam where an officer will ask you up to 20 questions from a list of 128. You must answer 12 to pass. (USCIS)
